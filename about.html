CHRONOLOGICAL ARCHIVE

Our Story

Walk through the memories, achievements, and milestones that built our academy legacy.

Academy Beginning (2018)

Danzo began as a creative space dedicated to classical aesthetics and freestyle movement experimentation. Our goal was to create an environment where instruction matched the standards of premium theater shows.

The First Batch (2020)

We welcomed our first ensemble of dedicated students, focusing on foundation curves in ballet, traditional classical mudras, and raw hip-hop grooves.

Major Concert Performances (2022)

Danzo students performed on stage at national theaters, establishing our signature style of live theatrical stagecraft and lighting production.

Awards & Present Day (2026)

Awarded the Performing Arts Guild trophy for choreography excellence. Today, we continue to bridge classical precision and modern street energy.
